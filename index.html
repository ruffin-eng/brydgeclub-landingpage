Frequently asked questions

Everything You Need to Know

Who is Brydge Club for?
Accredited investors ready to make their first private angel investment. No prior angel investing experience required!
What are typical check sizes?
Our members can invest anywhere from $1K–$50K into Brydge-sourced deals. There's no minimum annual commitment — you invest only in the companies that fit your thesis.
What does a cohort look like?
Cohorts run for one month with weekly sessions covering everything you need to know to make your first investment with Brydge Club. We cover evaluating startups, investment terms, diligence checklists, and more!
Do I need to be an accredited investor?
Yes. Investments through Brydge are private placements, so members must qualify as accredited investors under SEC rules.
